About Us

We are a group of tech professionals working with schools to leverage technological solutions to enhance students' performance both inside and outside the classroom, with the help of teachers and parents.We have blended some of the most recent technologies to create a product that can make every activity more inclusive for every child in and out of the classroom.
Our goal is to improve and make every student more confident in their abilities because we believe every child is unique and has the potential to be the best if given the proper care, in our opinion. We think that if we can give school greater meaning, include parents who care, and create a unique plan for each child, they will succeed in their own unique way.

In order to provide schools and students with more advanced information, link-Park also strives to increase school understanding and importance of ICT. Any student with internet access can use Link-resources Park's because one of its key goals is to make its services more accessible, we also want to work with schools to create programs that are suitable for each student.
