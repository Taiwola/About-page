About Us

LinkPark is an edutech platform that is built to support the existing school system to bring out the best out of students.
At LinkPark, we believe that every student is special, the only reason why they struggle with their academics is the lack of special attention and support they need to become the star they are meant to be.
The physical school is is limited in bridging this gap because of its mass teaching method, and very minimal way of tracking student progress. However, with a fully digitalised system, students can be tracked, parents can be involved, and the gap can be bridged.
LinkPark is not a digital school, rather , it is a digital tool to help schools to help students.
LinkPark was founded in July, 2022, and headquartered at Akure, Ondo state. LinkPark team is led by Olanitori Tobi, a graduate of Computer Engineering from FUOYE, and a self taught Programmer.

LinkPark unlike most other tech platforms is not a distruptive solution, but a supportive solution to digitalise your school experience and as a result simplify education for you.
